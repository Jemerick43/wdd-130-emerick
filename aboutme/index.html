James Emerick

Hi! I'm currently a computer science major attending Brigham Young University, Idaho. I have some history with computers, which dates all the way back to when I was as young as ten years old. I've always enjoyed using computers for various things, from watching youtube videos to playing both "Vanilla" and "Modded" Minecraft (Vanilla being the base game, and Modded meaning playing a modified version of the game). As such, my time so far at BYUI has been nothing short of inspiring and insightful.

I have been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints for as long as I can remember. However, that doesn't mean that I have always believed in God or in Jesus. Through many challenges and difficulties that I have faced, I have found slowly but surely that God is real, and that He loves all of His children, and it is because of this that I hope to one day be the kind of person that He could be proud of.
